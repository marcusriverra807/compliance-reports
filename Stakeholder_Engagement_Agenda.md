# Stakeholder Engagement Agenda

## Purpose
To gather input from stakeholders regarding the automation of evidence gathering processes.

## Participants
- Compliance Officers
- IT Security Team
- Management Representatives

## Agenda
1. **Introductions** (10 minutes)
   - Brief introduction of participants and their roles.

2. **Overview of Automation Goals** (15 minutes)
   - Discuss the objectives of automating evidence gathering.

3. **Stakeholder Needs Assessment** (30 minutes)
   - Gather input on specific needs and expectations from each stakeholder group.
   - Discuss current challenges and gaps in the existing processes.

4. **Requirements Discussion** (30 minutes)
   - Identify functional and non-functional requirements for the automation tools.
   - Explore any integration needs with existing systems.

5. **Next Steps and Timeline** (15 minutes)
   - Outline the timeline for the implementation phases.
   - Discuss follow-up meetings and communication plans.

6. **Q&A Session** (20 minutes)
   - Open the floor for any additional questions or comments from participants.

## Conclusion
Summarize key takeaways and action items from the discussion.
