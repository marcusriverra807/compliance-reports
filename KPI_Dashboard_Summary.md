# KPI Dashboard Presentation: Summary of Key Changes

## 1. Access Control Enhancements
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** 
  - Implemented RBAC to restrict access based on user roles, enhancing overall security.
- **Regular Permission Reviews:** 
  - Established a systematic process for ongoing reviews of access permissions, ensuring that only authorized users retain access.

## 2. Data Encryption Measures
- **Adoption of Strong Encryption Protocols:** 
  - Utilized AES-256 for data at rest and TLS 1.2 or higher for data in transit, ensuring the protection of sensitive information.
- **Secure Key Management Practices:** 
  - Implemented secure management and regular rotation of encryption keys to mitigate risks associated with key compromise.

## 3. Vulnerability Management Strategy
- **Commitment to Regular Vulnerability Assessments:** 
  - Conducting quarterly vulnerability scans and penetration tests to proactively identify and address security weaknesses.
- **Timely Software Updates:** 
  - Emphasized prompt patching and updating of all software and systems to ensure protection against known threats.

## 4. Monitoring and Compliance Tracking
- **Establishment of Compliance Monitoring Systems:** 
  - Created systems to continuously track compliance status and generate regular reports on compliance levels and regulatory changes.
- **Continuous Staff Training Programs:** 
  - Scheduled ongoing training for staff on PCI-DSS requirements and best security practices to ensure readiness and compliance with evolving regulations.
