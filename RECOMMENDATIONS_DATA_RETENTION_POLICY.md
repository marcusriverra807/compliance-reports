# Recommendations for DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md Review

Based on the recent review of the updated DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md file, the following recommendations and comments are provided to address potential issues and areas needing clarification:

1. **Key Management Documentation**
   - Clarify or provide a reference/link to the documented procedures for key management, including key generation, distribution, rotation, and access control.

2. **MFA Implementation Details**
   - Specify preferred or currently implemented MFA methods/tools aligned with NIST SP 800-63B to clarify practical implementation.

3. **Audit Log Retention Criteria**
   - Define specific criteria or examples that determine when audit logs need to be retained longer than the minimum two years period.

4. **Vulnerability Testing Scope and Standards**
   - Clarify the scope, tools, and standards/frameworks used for quarterly vulnerability assessments and penetration tests.

5. **Incident Response Plan Reference**
   - Add a reference or link to the detailed incident response plan document covering timelines, escalation paths, and communication plans.

6. **Employee Training Effectiveness**
   - Describe methods for measuring and tracking the effectiveness and completion of employee training programs.

7. **Third-Party Vendor Risk Management**
   - Provide examples of risk assessment criteria and monitoring frequency for third-party vendors handling sensitive data.

8. **Policy Review Responsibilities and Communication**
   - Specify the roles or teams responsible for conducting the policy reviews and how changes are communicated to stakeholders.

Implementing these recommendations will improve the clarity, completeness, and enforceability of the Data Retention Policy.
