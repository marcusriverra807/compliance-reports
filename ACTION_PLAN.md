## PCI-DSS Compliance Action Plan

### Compliance Improvements Action Plan
1. **Appoint Compliance Officer**: Designate a compliance officer to oversee PCI-DSS compliance.
2. **Conduct a Compliance Audit**: Assess current compliance status against PCI-DSS requirements.
3. **Develop Security Controls**:
   - Implement role-based access control (RBAC).
   - Establish data encryption protocols for both data at rest and in transit.
4. **Schedule Vulnerability Scans**: Conduct regular vulnerability scans and penetration tests at least quarterly.
5. **Update Documentation**: Ensure all compliance documentation reflects current practices and gaps.
6. **Training Program**: Create an ongoing training program for employees on PCI-DSS compliance and security best practices.
7. **Monitoring System**: Establish a monitoring system to track compliance status and report any changes in regulatory requirements.
8. **Regular Reviews**: Schedule regular reviews of compliance status and update the action plan as needed.

### Timeline
- Complete remediation within 6 months with regular reviews to ensure compliance with regulatory updates.
