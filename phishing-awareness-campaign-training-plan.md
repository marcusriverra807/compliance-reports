# Phishing Awareness Campaign Training Program Plan

## 1. Objectives
- Increase staff awareness of phishing tactics and indicators.
- Provide practical guidance on how to handle suspicious emails and links.
- Conduct simulated phishing exercises to reinforce learning.

## 2. Training Schedule and Timelines
- Initial Rollout: Starting July 1, 2026
  - Develop training materials and resources: May 1 - June 15, 2026
  - Review and finalize training materials: June 16 - June 30, 2026
  - Schedule and send invitations for training sessions: June 10 - June 20, 2026
  - Conduct initial training sessions: July 1 - July 31, 2026
- Follow-up Refresher Sessions: Quarterly, starting October 1, 2026
  - Plan and schedule refresher sessions: 2 weeks prior to each quarter
  - Conduct refresher sessions: First month of each quarter

## 3. Resources Needed
- Training materials (presentations, guides, videos)
- Simulated phishing exercise platform/tools
- Training coordinators and IT security team collaboration
- Communication channels for invitations and follow-ups
- Feedback collection mechanism (surveys, forms)

## 4. Participants
- All staff members across departments
- IT Security Team for simulated exercises
- Training coordinators and facilitators

## 5. Action Items and Responsibilities
- Develop training materials and resources: Training Team (May 1 - June 15)
- Review and finalize training materials: Training Team and Security Lead (June 16 - June 30)
- Schedule and conduct training sessions: Training Coordinators (June 10 - July 31)
- Coordinate simulated phishing exercises: IT Security Team and Training Team (Ongoing)
- Track participation and gather feedback: Training Coordinators (Ongoing)
