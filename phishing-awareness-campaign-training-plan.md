# Phishing Awareness Campaign Training Plan

## Timeline and Task List

### Phase 1: Preparation (May 1 - June 15, 2026)
- Identify and engage key stakeholders (Security Awareness Lead, IT Security Team, HR/Training, Compliance)
- Gather existing training materials and resources
- Research latest phishing tactics and indicators
- Draft initial training content and simulation scenarios

### Phase 2: Development (June 16 - June 30, 2026)
- Finalize training materials and resources
- Develop simulated phishing exercises
- Review and approve training content with stakeholders
- Prepare communication plan for staff announcement

### Phase 3: Initial Rollout (July 1 - July 31, 2026)
- Announce training program to all staff
- Schedule and conduct initial training sessions
- Launch simulated phishing exercises
- Track attendance and participation
- Collect feedback from participants

### Phase 4: Evaluation and Improvement (August 1 - August 15, 2026)
- Analyze feedback and participation data
- Identify areas for content improvement
- Update training materials as needed

### Phase 5: Ongoing Training (Quarterly, starting October 1, 2026)
- Schedule and conduct quarterly refresher training sessions
- Continue simulated phishing exercises
- Monitor participation and effectiveness
- Update content based on emerging threats and feedback

---

## Responsibilities
- Security Awareness Lead: Overall project management, stakeholder coordination
- IT Security Team: Provide technical input, assist with simulations
- HR/Training Department: Schedule sessions, manage communications
- Compliance Team: Ensure training meets regulatory requirements

## Tracking and Reporting
- Maintain attendance records
- Collect and summarize feedback
- Provide regular progress reports to management

---

This plan will ensure organized implementation and effective tracking of the phishing awareness campaign training program.
