# Joint Detection Rule Review Session Agenda

## Objective
To collaboratively review, tune, and prioritize detection rules based on recent red team findings and simulated attacks to improve detection accuracy and compliance adherence.

## Agenda Items

1. Welcome and Introductions
2. Review of Recent Red Team Findings
- Summary of key attack techniques and vulnerabilities identified
- Impact on current detection rules

3. Detection Rule Performance Review
- Discussion of false positives and missed detections
- Identification of gaps in detection coverage

4. Prioritization of Rule Adjustments
- Agree on rules to tune, add, or retire
- Assign responsibilities and timelines

5. Compliance Alignment
- Ensure detection rules meet PCI-DSS and other regulatory requirements

6. Next Steps and Scheduling
- Plan for follow-up sessions and continuous improvement

7. Q&A and Open Discussion

---

Please come prepared with relevant detection rule data and findings for productive collaboration.
