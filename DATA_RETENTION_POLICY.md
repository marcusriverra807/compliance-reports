# Data Retention Policy

## Purpose
This Data Retention Policy establishes the requirements for retaining, classifying, and disposing of data within the organization to ensure compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and standards.

## Scope
This policy applies to all data generated, received, or maintained by the organization in all formats and media.

## Data Classification
Data shall be classified into the following categories to determine retention requirements:
- **Confidential:** Sensitive data requiring strict access controls and secure disposal.
- **Internal Use Only:** Data intended for internal operations with moderate sensitivity.
- **Public:** Data approved for public release with no restrictions on access.

## Retention Periods
Retention periods for data are based on legal, regulatory, and business requirements. Typical retention guidelines include:
- **Confidential Data:** Retain for a minimum of 7 years or as required by law.
- **Internal Use Only Data:** Retain for at least 3 years unless otherwise specified.
- **Public Data:** Retain as long as necessary for business purposes.

## Data Disposal
Data no longer required shall be disposed of securely to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure:
- **Confidential Data:** Use secure destruction methods such as shredding, degaussing, or certified data wiping.
- **Internal Use Only Data:** Use appropriate deletion methods ensuring data cannot be recovered.
- **Public Data:** Dispose of by standard deletion or recycling processes.

## Responsibilities
- Data Owners are responsible for classifying data and ensuring retention requirements are met.
- IT Department shall implement technical controls for data retention and disposal.
- Compliance Officer shall oversee adherence to this policy and update it as needed.

## Review and Updates
This policy shall be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to remain compliant with changing regulations and business needs.
